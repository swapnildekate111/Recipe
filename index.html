Grandma 's Lemon Meringue Pie

One 9-inch pie
30 Min - Prep time
10 Min - Cook time
40 Min - Total
8 Servings

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 2 tbsp all-purpose flour
  • 3 tbsp cornstarch
  • 1/4 tbsp salt
  • 1 1/2 cup water
  • 2 lemons, juiced and zested
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 4 egg yolks, beaten
  • 1 (9 inch) pie crust, baked
  • 4 egg white/li>
  • 6 tbsp white sugar
